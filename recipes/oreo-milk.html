Oreo Milkshake

Description:

This milkshake is embedded with oreo crumbs which enhances the flavor almost ten fold! Enjoy the lovely weather with this classic timeless milkshake.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Oreos
  • Ice Cream
  • Cool Whip

Steps:

  1. Combine ice cream, milk and divided Oreos in blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Top with Cool Whip Free.
